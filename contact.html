Frequently Asked Questions

How much do your services cost?

Every project is different, so we provide custom quotes based on your specific needs. We'll give you a clear, upfront price with no hidden fees. Most small business websites start around $2,500, and we offer payment plans.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed within 1-2 weeks. Simple sites can be done even faster. We'll give you a realistic timeline during our consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do I need technical knowledge to use your solutions?

Not at all! Everything we build is designed to be user-friendly. We provide training and documentation, and our support team is always available to help if you get stuck.

Can you work with my existing systems?

In most cases, yes. We can integrate with popular tools like QuickBooks, Google Workspace, and most CRM systems. We'll discuss your current setup during our consultation.

What if I need changes after the project is done?

We provide ongoing support and maintenance for all our projects. Small updates are usually included, and larger changes are handled quickly at reasonable rates.

Do you only work with contractors?

While we specialize in trades and service businesses, we work with any small business that needs simple, effective digital solutions. If you're not sure if we're a good fit, just ask!
